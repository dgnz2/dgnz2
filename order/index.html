Requesting an image in 3 simple steps

  1. Visit an artist's monograph webpage via books.zedign.com
    • For example, to see what images are available from Vincent Van Gogh - Paintings & Drawings - Vol 2 , you'd visit its webpage here
  2. Once you're there, obtain the eBook edition, browse through it, and note down the Page number & title of your desired image in that book
    • For example, Page 5, Pair of Shoes Paris 1887
  3. Return to this website and use the "Open Image Request Form" button below (or click here), and submit it. That's all!

Once the product is ready, you will be notified. Then all you have to do is to visit the product's page provided and purchase it there, if you'd like. Yup, that's right, there is no obligation whatsoever to actually buy it if you don't like it, or if you just change your mind for another image. In which case, just request another image, without obligation. Do it again until you get the right one.
